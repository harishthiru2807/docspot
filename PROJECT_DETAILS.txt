DocSpot Project Report: Full Documentation
==========================================

1. Abstract
-----------
DocSpot is a decentralized, AI-driven healthcare platform designed to bridge the rural-urban medical divide. By integrating a bilingual (Tamil & English) AI Video Doctor, role-based clinical dashboards, and real-time inventory synchronization, DocSpot provides a seamless healthcare journey from symptom onset to medication fulfillment.

2. Introduction
---------------
Healthcare accessibility in India is hampered by a significant shortage of specialists in rural areas and the presence of profound linguistic barriers in clinical communication. DocSpot introduces a multi-stakeholder platform that connects Patients, Doctors, Nurses, and Pharmacies through a single, synchronized API layer.

3. Objectives
-------------
- Accessibility: Provide 24/7 AI medical guidance in Tamil/English.
- Stakeholder Integration: Synchronize clinical data across all portals.
- Trust: Humanize AI through a realistic digital human avatar (Dr. ARIA).
- Data Digitization: Secure, QR-based digital health IDs.

4. Technical Stack
-----------------
Frontend: React.js & Vite
Backend: Node.js & Express
Security: JWT (JSON Web Tokens)
API Layer: Axios with Interceptors

5. Functional Modules
--------------------
Patient Portal: AI Diagnosis, QR Profile, Live Vitals.
Doctor Portal: Queue Management, Video Consultation, E-Prescription.
Nurse Portal: Ward Oversight, Clinical Alerting.
Pharmacy Portal: Stock Compliance, Fulfillment Queue.

6. Innovation: AI Video Doctor (Dr. ARIA)
---------------------------------------
Uses a custom CSS lip-sync engine coordinated with the Web Speech API to provide a high-trust, natural interaction for preliminary diagnoses.

7. Conclusion
-------------
DocSpot demonstrates how AI and modern web architecture can solve real-world healthcare accessibility problems by providing a scalable, futuristic model for national healthcare delivery.
